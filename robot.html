Kickoff & Game Analysis

The season officially began with the game reveal. The team broke into sub-groups to study the game manual, score possibilities, and draft initial robot concepts. Three drivetrain configurations were evaluated.

[STRATEGY]

Prototype & Iteration

Cardboard and plywood prototypes were built for the intake and scoring systems. The linear slide extension was iterated three times to hit the target cycle time. CAD modelling began in Onshape.

[MECHANICAL] [CAD]

Robot Build & Wiring

Full metal assembly completed over two build weekends. The electrical harness was colour-coded for rapid field repairs. First successful drive under driver control achieved.

[BUILD] [ELECTRICAL]

Software & Autonomous Tuning

Pedro Pathing spline paths were tuned for two autonomous routes: a high-scoring five-sample path and a reliable two-sample fallback. PID coefficients for each motor group were version-controlled on GitHub.
