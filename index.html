About Us

Who Are The Cyclodials?

Cyclodials is a FIRST Tech Challenge (FTC) robotics team passionate about engineering, programming, and community outreach. We design, build, and program competitive robots to tackle seasonal challenges — and we learn something new at every step of the process.

Based out of Irvington High School, our team brings together students with diverse skills: mechanical design, software development, CAD, and strategy. Together, we build more than robots — we build problem-solvers.

We compete in the INTO THE DEEP season and are driven by FIRST's mission to inspire young people to become science and technology leaders.

// Innovation

We don't just follow the playbook — we rewrite it. Creative engineering is at the heart of everything we build.

// Gracious Professionalism

We compete hard and support harder. Win or lose, we lift up every team we meet on the field.

// Continuous Improvement

Every match, every iteration, every line of code is a chance to be better than yesterday's version of ourselves.

// Community

We host workshops, mentor younger teams, and share what we know — because a rising tide lifts all robots.
