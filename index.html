Engineering Human Potential

The Cyclodials mission is to empower the next generation of engineers through the rigorous pursuit of mechanical excellence and collaborative innovation. We don't just build robots — we build the architectural foundation for future technological leadership.

Based at Irvington High School, our team specializes in high-torque cycloidal drive systems and modular software frameworks that push the boundaries of the FIRST Tech Challenge.

// KINETIC EFFICIENCY

Every rotation counts. We optimize for maximum torque with minimal mechanical loss using custom cycloidal geometry.

// ALGORITHMIC AGILITY

Implementing advanced computer vision and PID control systems for autonomous precision during match play.

// RADICAL ITERATION

Rapid prototyping via additive manufacturing allows us to test and deploy hardware updates in record time.

// OPEN SYSTEMS

Mentoring others by open-sourcing our design documentation and fostering a transparent STEM ecosystem.

Team Lineage & Evolution

2022

The Genesis

Founded as a rookie team under the Kinetic Cyber-Systems banner. Focused on mastering drivetrain fundamentals and basic game mechanics. Secured 'Rookie Inspiration' award in our debut regional.

2023

CenterStage Shift

Introduced our first custom cycloidal drivetrain. Optimized for rapid intake and pixel placement. Finalist Alliance member at the State Championship and recipient of the 'Design Award'.

2024

Deep Sea Tech

Current deployment. Developing advanced suspension systems for arena obstacles and high-precision vertical expansion modules. Integrating AI for real-time field navigation.
